The Best Movies According to Kelcie

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

The Nightmare Before Christmas

It can be considered both a Halloween and Christmas movie. It also has amazing music and dynamic characters.

Pride and Prejudice

It has some of the most beautiful scenery and costumes. The storyline is similar to love stories that happen in current times. And I love that when they speak it sounds like poetry.

My Big Fat Greek Wedding 2

This movie is so heart warming and is there when I need to smile. It shows a different culture, while offering romance and laughter. Not to mention it reminds women that we have a purpose outside of being wives and mothers.
